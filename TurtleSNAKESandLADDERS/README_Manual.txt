When the game launches you will be asked to enter the type of your character, 
there are 5 in total, three of which are my addition.
Each character can only be selected ONCE.

You will press enter to roll the dice.Game is a classic ladder and snakes.
When a person wins you will be asked a y or n question which restarts the gameplay or closes it.
